When you interact with বাংলার সহায়ক via WhatsApp, we collect:

Your information is used solely to:

Your complaint details may be shared with relevant government departments and officials for the purpose of resolving your grievance. We do not sell or share your personal data with any third party for commercial purposes.

All data is stored securely. We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access.

Complaint data is retained for a period necessary to resolve your grievance and for record-keeping purposes as required by applicable law.
